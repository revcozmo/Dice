Rain Bets

Enter a number of bets and amount before you click the color to rain on. The rain counter will display how many bets are left. Bets will automatically stop if your balance is less than a bet. If you don't want to rain, just rain 1 (default).

For strategic rains, use M4RT-N the gambit bot. Tell it what you'd like to do if the coin lands in each slot; you can choose to do nothing, multiply your wager by a number, or set your wager to the base. M4RT-N knows the outcome of your coin before it falls, so if you use fast rain mode be ready for the consequences! Setting a good limit is always important. Play with care!

Min/Max bet

The minimum you can wager is 100 satoshi (1 bit) on any line. The maximum you can wager is determined by potential profit, which is currently limited to 5 bitcoins (5,000,000 bits) on any wager.

Odds

The odds are calculated using standard probability distribution. This type of game and calculation is fully explained in the Odds section of the Menu on LuckyBit; this version uses 8 rows instead of 16 and so has five different probability ranges instead of nine. The chances of hitting each slot are exactly:

  • Fringe: 2:256=0.78125%
  • 1 away: 16:256=6.25%
  • 2 away: 56:256=21.875%
  • 3 away: 112:256=43.75%
  • Center: 70:256=27.34375%

The numbers for each line are selected so that the house edge is exactly 0.78125%.
